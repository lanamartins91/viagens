Lençóis Maranhenses

An unmissable paradise!

The fine white sand marks the paradisiacal path taken by travelers through the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park. The freshwater lagoons between the dunes form a scenery that is unique in the world and it is impossible not to be thrilled when you reach the top of the landscape and see the immensity of the Lençóis Maranhenses. Located in the northwest of Maranhão and 250 km from the capital São Luís, the Lençóis Maranhenses attract tourists in search of the unique phenomenon of interdune lagoons by the sea. And the best thing about the Lençóis Maranhenses is that, with each new patch of water between the dunes, the feeling of being in paradise is repeated. Are you planning a trip to Lençóis Maranhenses or do you dream of going there? Get ready to discover one of the most incredible places in Brazil and the world!
When traveling to the Lençóis Maranhenses, you need to keep an eye on the calendar to see the phenomenon of the lagoons in the middle of the dunes. The high season in Lençóis Maranhenses is during and just after the rains, especially between April and August. At the height of the drought, from October to December, almost all the lagoons disappear and the landscape, full of water, turns into an immense desert. The truth is, however, that Lençóis Maranhenses is beautiful all year round, whether the lagoons are full or not.
